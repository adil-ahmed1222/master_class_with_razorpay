**Step 1 — Webhook** (kind label: Trigger)

- This is where everything begins.
- Whenever a lead submits a form, the information is instantly received by your AI Employee.
- Think of it as the entry point of the workflow.
- Closing: Scroll down to see what happens next.

**Step 2 — AI Agent** (kind label: AI)

- The AI analyzes the lead.
- It understands:
  - Name
  - Company
  - Requirements
  - Priority
- Closing: Then it decides what action should happen next. Instead of manually reviewing every lead, the AI Employee does it automatically.

**Step 3 — Telegram Notification** (kind label: Notify)

- The AI instantly informs your team.
- You receive:
  - Lead Score
  - Priority Level
  - Suggested Action
- Closing: You get this within seconds. No manual follow-up is required.

**Step 4 — Email Generator** (kind label: Action)

- The AI creates a personalized email.
- Every message is generated using:
  - Lead Information
  - Business Context
  - Desired Outcome
- Closing: Each email is unique.

**Step 5 — Gmail Draft** (kind label: Output)

- The draft is automatically prepared.
- Your AI Employee has now:
  - Received the lead
  - Analyzed the lead
  - Notified the team
  - Written the email
  - Created the draft
- Closing: without any manual work.
